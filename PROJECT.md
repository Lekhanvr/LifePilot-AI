# LifePilot AI

## Vision

LifePilot AI is an AI-powered Personal Operating System.

Instead of opening multiple apps for planning, notes, decisions, and productivity, users interact with one intelligent AI assistant.

---

# Problem Statement

People constantly switch between apps like Notes, Calendar, To-do Lists, ChatGPT, and Google Search.

LifePilot AI combines these experiences into one intelligent assistant.

---

# Target Users

- Professionals
- Job Seekers
